Einstein's Photoelectric Equation:
$$eV_0 = h\nu - \Phi$$
The graph plots $V_0$ on the Y-axis and $\nu$ on the X-axis. This forms a straight line equation $y = mx + c$, where the slope $m = h/e$.
Calculate slope $m$ from the drawn green line to find $h = m \cdot e$.
