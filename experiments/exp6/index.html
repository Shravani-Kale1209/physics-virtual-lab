Physics Formulation
Babinet's Principle
Diffraction by a hair (thickness a) produces the identical intensity pattern as a single slit of width a.
First Minima Condition
a · sin(θ) = 1 · λ
For small θ, sin(θ) ≈ y / D

Total fringe spread (β = 2y):
a = (2 · λ · D) / β
Instructions: Use the zoomed ruler to measure β (the distance between the two first-order dark fringes bounding the central bright spot).
