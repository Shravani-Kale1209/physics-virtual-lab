Physics Reference
Diffraction Equation
$d \sin(\theta) = m \lambda$

Where $d$ is the grating spacing ($1/N$).
$m$ is the order of the maxima (we measure $m=1$).

Geometry of Screen
$\tan(\theta) = \frac{y_1}{D}$

By measuring the distance format the central maxima to the 1st order spot ($y_1$) and knowing the screen distance ($D$), we calculate $\theta$, which gives the experimental wavelength $\lambda$.
