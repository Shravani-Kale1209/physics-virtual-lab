Physics Reference
$$V_H = \frac{I \cdot B}{n \cdot e \cdot t}$$ $$R_H = \frac{V_H \cdot t}{I \cdot B}$$ Where $R_H$ is the Hall Coefficient, $n$ is charge carrier concentration, $e$ is electron charge, and $t$ is the sample thickness.
